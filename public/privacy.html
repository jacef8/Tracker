GroundLink Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

GroundLink ("we", "our", or "us") operates the GroundLink mobile application and website. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Name: The display name you enter when joining a group. This name is visible to other members of your group.
  • Location: Real-time GPS location while you are active in a group session. Location is shared only with members of your group and is not stored permanently after your session ends.
  • Group activity: Pins you drop, chat messages you send, and your session timestamps are stored in your group's shared session data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your position on the shared map to members of your group
  • To enable real-time group chat and shared pin drops
  • To show your name and last-seen time to group members

Data Sharing

Your location, name, pins, and messages are shared only with members of the group you have joined. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Group session data is stored using Google Firebase Realtime Database. Data is associated with your group name and session. Group administrators can delete group data at any time through the admin panel.

Data Retention

Active session data persists while your group is active. Location data is removed when your session ends or after a period of inactivity. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us or by asking a group administrator to clear the group data.

Location Data

GroundLink requests access to your device's GPS location while the app is in use. Location data is transmitted in real time to other members of your group only. We do not store historical location tracks permanently.

Children's Privacy

GroundLink is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS encrypted connections. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
