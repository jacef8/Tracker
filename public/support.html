Common Questions

How do I share my location with my group?
Join or create a room from the Home screen, and your location shares automatically with everyone else in that room.

Why isn't push-to-talk working?
Make sure GroundLink has microphone access in your device's Settings, and that you have an internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular).

How do I stop sharing my location?
Leave the room from the map screen, or turn off location sharing from the Settings menu.
